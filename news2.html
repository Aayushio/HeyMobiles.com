iQOO 13 Amazon availability confirmed ahead
of India launch

By Ishan Agarwal -Published On: November 1, 2024

Highlights

  • iQOO 13 is one of the first Snapdragon 8 Elite devices launching in India.
  • The phone is confirmed to feature a new light ring around the camera bump and a BMW Motorsport variant like its predecessor.
  • Based on new teasers, the device will be available exclusively though Amazon in the country.

iQOO 13 India launch is expected soon and Amazon has now confirmed that the flagship will be available on its platform. The iQOO 13 will be one of the first devices to launch with the Snapdragon 8 Elite chipset in the country. iQOO has already teased a partnership with BMW Motorsport for this launch, like its predecessor, and now it has also confirmed where the device will be available for sale.

iQOO 13 Amazon India details

iQOO has kept its phones exclusive to Amazon in India. iQOO 13 is no exception, as the teaser page for the upcoming phone is now live on the e-commerce site. The pre-heat images with the “G.O.A.T (Greatest of all Time) Performance” tagline hint at the phone being the same as the Chinese device variant with a light ring around the camera bump, Snapdragon 8 Elite chipset and a BMW Motorsport variant.

iQOO 13 is not the only Snapdragon 8 Elite-powered device launching through Amazon in India, as the Realme GT 7 Pro is expected to debut through the same platform later this month.
