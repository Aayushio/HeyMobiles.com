Motorola Edge 50 Pro 5G

Complete Specifications

Verdict

The Motorola Edge 50 Pro demands attention, despite a few shortcomings. The phone`s front camera is not the best in class, and the battery life is relatively underwhelming. However, the smartphone makes up for these with its eye-catching design, fluent display, impressive camera setup, and premium features such as wireless charging support, lightning-fast wired charging speeds, and solid build quality.
